Nicolas GUILLARD

Since early 2023, I have been passionately immersed in the field of AI, after re-immersing myself in it in 2020 during the Covid period, and first coming into contact with it during my studies. I consider it to be one of humanity's greatest scientific adventures. But exploring it, like other fields, can lead to both great benefits and catastrophic calamities. Understanding how AI models work, being aware of the risks and challenges, and mastering their use are essential pillars for getting the most out of them with confidence, helping us solve our current problems and meet our future challenges, understand the world, and, why not, help us better understand ourselves and become a better humanity.

That is why I am interested in the topics of interpretability and evaluation in the context of AI safety.
